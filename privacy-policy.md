# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 30, 2026**

Aytac Arslan ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Tastamp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Photos**: Meal photos you capture within the App are stored locally on your device.
- **Location Data**: When you grant permission, we collect your device's location to tag your food entries with restaurant locations. This data is stored locally on your device.
- **Notes and Ratings**: Any text notes or ratings you add to your food entries are stored locally on your device.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Advertising Data**: Our third-party advertising partners may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), IP address, and general usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See "Third-Party Advertising" below for details.

## How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the App's core features (photo capture, location tagging, food collection).
- To display advertisements within the App.
- To improve the App experience.

## Data Storage
All your food entries, photos, locations, and notes are stored **locally on your device**. We do not upload or store your personal food collection data on any external server.

## Third-Party Advertising
We use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display ads within the App. These services may collect and use certain information about your device and usage patterns to provide targeted or non-targeted advertisements.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
- **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads

## Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob** — for serving advertisements
- **OpenStreetMap** — for map tile rendering

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

## Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights
Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete any food entry at any time, or uninstall the App to remove all associated data from your device.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App or on this page.
